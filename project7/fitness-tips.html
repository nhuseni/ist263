Fitness Tips That Actually Work

Start Small

Consistency matters more than intensity. Short walks, light workouts, or stretching help build lasting habits.

Find What You Enjoy

Fitness should feel enjoyable, not forced. Trying different activities helps you stay motivated.

Rest and Recover

Recovery is essential. Rest days support both physical and mental health.
